6. Opt-Out Method

Opting out of receiving SMS messages can be done at any time by replying "STOP" to any SMS message received. Alternatively, direct contact can be made to request removal from the messaging list by calling (386) 456-0055 or emailing Carenowpharmacy@gmail.com. If SMS messages are not desired, the SMS consent box on forms can be left unchecked.

7. Help and Support

For any issues with SMS messaging, reply with the keyword HELP to any message. Alternatively, help can be obtained directly from us at (386) 456-0055 or Carenowpharmacy@gmail.com.

All prescriptions must be valid and from licensed healthcare providers. We are committed to protecting your health information in accordance with HIPAA regulations. Your prescription and health information will be kept confidential and used only for legitimate healthcare purposes. Patient information will never be sold or shared for marketing purposes.

10. Payment Terms & Insurance

Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made. We accept most insurance plans and will work with you to maximize your benefits. Co-pays and deductibles are due at time of pickup. We also accept cash, credit cards, and HSA/FSA cards.

11. Refund & Return Policy

Prescription medications cannot be returned once dispensed due to safety and regulatory requirements. Refunds may be considered for billing errors or insurance processing issues. Over-the-counter items may be returned within 30 days with receipt.

12. Standard Messaging Disclosures

Message and data rates may apply. Opt out at any time by texting "STOP." For assistance, text "HELP" or visit our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy pages. Message frequency may vary.
